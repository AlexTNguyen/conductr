To start conducting your orchestra:

1. Enter a Playlist ID in the search bar above!



2. Set your swiping sensitivity. The more right it is, the more sensitive.

3. Swipe right to skip to the next song.

4. Swipe left to go back to the previous song.

5. Swipe up to play/pause the song.

6. Swipe down to shuffle to the next song.

Happy Conducting!
